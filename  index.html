Game Design

This project is a vibrant exploration of African music, meticulously aimed to showcase the diverse sounds of five countries representing different regions of the continent. Inspired by the richness of African heritage, particularly from West Africa, I has brought this concept to life through a physical representation of Africa using a map fixed to a chipboard plate, illuminated by LED strips for an immersive experience. The interactive element is realized through pushbuttons, each linked to a country's music selection, controlled by an Arduino board and Processing programming language. This innovative installation invites listeners to delve into the captivating rhythms and melodies of African cultures with a simple press.
